The constant barrage of gun restrictions from around the Nation put the burden squarely on the shoulders of the law-abiding citizen.  It has zero impact to the people that don't follow laws.  We are the ones with everything to loose. It doesn't make any sense! 

Gun laws can be tricky... it's up to us to figure them out. Even the enforcers of the laws don't always understand them. As of this minute, the states I mentioned in this video have the following magazine restrictions (verify for yourself, don't take my word for it).  There are a lot of nuances with these laws, and they are often never as clear as you would hope them to be.

Maryland:  No restriction on possession if otherwise legal to do so, but cannot purchase, sell, or transfer magazines with a capacity of more than 10

Washington DC: Cannot possess, sell, or transfer magazines with more than 10 round capacity

New York: This one is really fun... you can't possess a magazine with a capacity of more than 10 rounds, but you also can only load that magazine with no more than 7 rounds unless you are at a range.  Crazy, right? 

New Jersey: Illegal to posses magazine with more than 10 round capacity, and some really crazy ammunition restrictions.
