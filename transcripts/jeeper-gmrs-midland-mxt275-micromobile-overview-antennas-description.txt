KANSAS CITY, MO. (Jan. 7, 2020) – Leading two-way communications and weather radio manufacturer, Midland Radio, announced today a partnership with Jeep Jamboree as the official communications sponsor for the 2020 Jeep Jamboree Adventure Series.

Jeep Jamboree USA is a leader in the off-road event space, bringing outdoor-minded individuals together by hosting dozens of off-road adventure weekends across the country each year. Both
Midland Radio and Jeep Jamboree USA strive to connect people and encourage participation in the outdoors. As the official communications sponsor, Midland Radio will provide its proven,
category-leading MicroMobile and X-Talker GMRS radios to enhance on-trail navigation and communication, as well as event organization.

“We are thrilled to partner with such an active and well-respected organization,” said Andrew Kinsman, Digital Marketing Manager for Midland Radio. “We developed the MicroMobile GMRS radio line for those who require reliable, easy-to-use communication when off-roading and overlanding. The Jeep Jamboree USA community is a passionate group of off-roaders, dedicated to outdoor adventure. We see this partnership as an excellent opportunity for Midland to better connect with Jeep owners across the US.
Midland Radio products will also be available to consumers for purchase from the Jeep Jamboree USA website and official retail trailer at events. For more information on Jeep Jamboree USA events throughout 2020, visit www.jeepjamboreeusa.com.
