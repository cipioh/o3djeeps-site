This is the method for installing the Bartact (formerly TrekArmor) front JK seat covers.  My covers happen to be side air bag compatible, because my seats have air bags. The process is exactly the same for non-airbag compatible Bartact seat covers - without the flap of course. 

I find installation to be easy without using any aids such as tape or plastic bags. Feel free to use whatever method works best for you.  The key is to rotate the seat as far forward as it will go to open the space between the bottom cushion and the seat back.
