This was as pretty intense trail.  We started in low light fog conditions, and ended in night fog conditions. There were some pretty decent off-camber sections.  You can see me getting spotted over the crux (hardest) section after about 7:40 into the video.  Then, at about the 14:00 mark, you can see where I am being guided and winched over that same section in reverse...only this time in complete darkness.   Lights were actually less valuable because of the heavy fog, so I found that driving with my DRLs (which happen to be my front turn signal lamps) made for the best visibility.
