This is a 360 degree view of Black Bear Pass outside of Telluride Colorado. This is just from the steps through some of the switchbacks...which is the scenic and challenging part of the trail. 

Video never does this stuff justice, and this video is no different but hopefully you can see what the views are like. It was a fun trail, but not as scary as I expected... but those drop-offs are pretty insane in some places.
