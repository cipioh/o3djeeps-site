This is a very quick install video for the Barricade Rock Sliders from Extreme Terrain. It really is as simple as pulling the body mount bolts, putting in the sliders, and re-torquing the bolts. I, however, have a custom long arm suspension which required a little extra modification. It was really easy, even with that. I don't think I mentioned this in the video, but if you do any cutting on the sliders, make sure you repaint any exposed metal to protect against rust.
